Join us for an exciting in-person training event, the Live Coding Workshop - DS&AI Educators Training, on Fri Sep 29 2023 at 09:00:00 GMT+0100 (British Summer Time).

Our expert instructor, Inigo Aldazabal Mensa, is a qualified Carpentries Instructor Trainer and will guide you through general principle hands-on teaching coding examples, providing valuable insights and practical tips to engage your students effectively.

This workshop is part of the Leeds Institute for Teaching Excellence Project “Data Skills Literacy for Educators” and is designed specifically for educators interested in using coding examples while teaching their disciplines.

Hosted in the School of Mathematics, University of Leeds, at Woodhouse, LS2 9JT, this event offers a unique opportunity to enhance your teaching skills and learn innovative coding teaching techniques, based on The Carpentries format and resources.

Don't miss out on this incredible chance to network with like-minded professionals and stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in DS&AI education. Reserve your spot now! Deadline: 25/09/2023 at 3 pm BST
